FAQ

HOW TO ORDER?

Simply pick then fill up the order form with your valid email and contact number at https://www.sdc.com then go directly to the product section. Check your inbox for the order summary with your order number and payment instructions.

WHAT IF I WANT TO CANCEL MY ORDER?

You may email your request to sdc@gmail.com. However, please note that this is subject for approval and may only be approved if your order is still at the "PENDING" phase, and has not yet been processed.

Unfortunately, if the order has already been marked as "PROCESSED", we are unable to cancel it. This also means that the invoice is final and can no longer be modified

I WANT TO EDIT THE ITEMS IN MY ORDER, IS THIS POSSIBLE?

All orders are final upon placement, we cannot modify the quantity and/or the item on your order.

WHAT ARE YOUR STORE HOURS?

Our operating hours at Divisoria, Manila are from 12:30 PM until 8:30 PM every day. On the other hand, our branch at Divisoria 999 Mall 2 ( Pasilio 2D-12 ) operates from 10:00 AM to 10:00 PM, offering extended hours for your convenience.

WHERE ARE YOU LOCATED?

Our Flagship store can be found at Divisoria 999 Mall 2 ( Pasilio 2D-12 ) Divisoria, Manila. You'll have no trouble locating us using Waze or Google Maps. Simply search for "Social District Clothing" and you'll be on your way!.
